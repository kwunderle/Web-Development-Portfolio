Mobile Web Design Principles

  • Home
  • Accessibility

Navigation

A well designed web site will be easy to navigate on any screen big or small. The menus should be clearly defined and make any part of the page easily accessible.

Information

All the content on your page should be organized in a way that flows organically and makes sense to the user. The text should be styled in a way with font, size, and color so that it is comfortable to read

Size

People view the world wide web on a variety of devices and it is important that the look of your site reflects that. The user experience on a smart phone versus that of a desktop computer with a large monitor can be vastly different.

Touch Design

When designing the look of a mobile site, you should remember that it is going to be navigated with a finger instead of a mouse and/or keyboard. It is important to have adequate spacing inbetween interactive elements so that they are easy to click on with a fingertip.
