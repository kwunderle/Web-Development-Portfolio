About

Hello there and welcome to my Web Development class portfolio page. When I made this, I was a student at Lorain County Community College studying Web and Software Development. I have a background in art and this was part of my journey to add the functionality of creating a good looking and efficient working website to my repertoire. I have experience with photography and graphic design, and my favorite thing to do is make digital cartoons and comics. In my free time I enjoy making costumes and props and competing in craftmanship competitions at pop culture conventions. I would like to eventually use my education to make apps geared towards these sorts of hobbies. Check out some things I learned:
